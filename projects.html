MK7 GTI Brake Ducts

3D Printed ASA Brake Ducts

  • Designed and printed brake ducts for my GTI in ASA in order to cool the brake rotors. I have a TTRS Big Brake Kit installed on my car and the OEM rotors are known to heat up due to the bad rotor fin design. In order to save myself some money, instead of buying $1k 2 piece rotors, I built these ducts to cool the brakes for spirited drives and track days. I have the 2017 Sport trim which means there are no fog lights on the bumper so I took advantage of the extra space.

Camera Slider

Camera Slider for B-Roll Shots

MK7 GTI Project Car

Project Car for Fun Drives and Track Days

  • I am very passionate about cars and driving and this is my project car that I built for canyon runs and track days. I have installed multiple aftermarket parts in order to improve the overall performance of the car and it drives like a completely different machine. To name a few of the upgrades that I have installed myself include Bilstein B14 coilovers, TTRS BBK, Whiteline rear sway bar, Whiteline LCA, 034 roll center ball joints, Powerflex rear trailing arm bushing and dogbone insert, and many many more parts.

Car Data

Web Scraper to Learn Something New

  • Created a Jupyter Notebook in order to scrape the web for car data to learn something that I didn't know before. I used Beautiful Soup and Seaborn to scrape and visualize data from autoevolution's website.

Smart Parking

Real Time Parking Monitor for CSUDH

  • Senior project where we had to come up with an app that we haven't seen before. This app takes a livestream and gives real time updates on which parking spots were open/taken. This demo was our school parking lot which was always busy.

Toros Nav

Navigation App for Finding Classes

  • Worked with a group to build an Android application with GPS and Wifi waypoints to be able to find your class at CSUDH. The campus isn't that big but it can still be confusing to find your class and GPS only works well outside. By using a 3rd party API, I was able to map out the school with waypoints and wifi points which made finding your class much easier.
